El Shadai Primary School Kasubi
Admissions Requirements (Print Copy)
-----------------------------------

To complete an application, please provide:

1. Completed application form
   - Available from the school office or via our contact form.

2. Copy of the child's birth certificate

3. Previous school reports (where applicable)

4. Passport-size photograph of the child

5. Completed medical form
   - Provided by the school.

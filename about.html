About Us

Our story, mission, and the people behind El Shadai Primary School Kasubi

Our Story

A School Built on Care and Excellence

El Shadai Primary School Kasubi was founded in Kasubi with a simple belief: every child deserves a nurturing, safe, and stimulating environment where they can grow into confident, curious learners. What started as a small nursery has grown into a full primary school, serving families across Kampala and beyond.

We are proud of our roots in the community and our commitment to quality education that respects each child's unique potential. Our experienced staff, modern facilities, and strong values continue to make El Shadai Primary School Kasubi a trusted choice for parents who want the best start for their children.

What We Stand For

Mission, Vision & Values

🎯

Our Mission

To provide a holistic, inclusive education that nurtures every child's academic, social, and emotional growth in a safe and caring environment.

🌟

Our Vision

To be the leading nursery and primary school in Wakiso, recognised for excellence in teaching, character development, and community impact.

💚

Our Values

Integrity, respect, creativity, and collaboration guide everything we do. We believe in partnership between school, parents, and the community.
